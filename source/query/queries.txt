=================
View Past Queries
=================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

Compass automatically stores up to 20 most recent queries for each
collection.

To view these queries:

#. Click the :icon:`ellipsis-h` in the query bar
#. Click :guilabel:`Toggle Query History`.
